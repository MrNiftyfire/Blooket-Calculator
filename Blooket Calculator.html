Blooket Pack Calculator

Welcome to your friendly Blooket Pack Chance Calculator — the smart tool that helps you figure out your odds of pulling a specific blook when you open multiple packs! 🎲📦✨

Blooks are the collectible stars of Blooket — from common crowd-pleasers 🐥 to elusive legendaries 👑 — and knowing your chances makes opening packs even more fun (and less mysterious). Use this calculator to plan your pack openings, celebrate your luck 🍀, or just satisfy your curiosity about probabilities 📊.

How it helps:
• See the probability of getting at least one of a chosen blook after X packs. • Compare different pack counts to decide whether to save or open now. • Perfect for collectors tracking progress toward that one rare blook! 🧭🏆

💡 Click, enter your blook rarity and number of packs, and the calculator will show your odds — simple, fast, and fun. Ready to test your luck and level up your collection? 🚀🎉

To find out how many packs you can open, use the calculator:
1. Divide the total number of tokens you have by the number of tokens required for one pack.
Example:
If you have 2000 tokens and each Bug Pack costs 25 tokens,
2000 ÷ 25 = 80 packs
